Welcome

This is my espresso website. I love everything related to coffee, and I spend far more time and money in the world of coffee than any reasonable person could, should, or would. My favorite form of coffee, by far, is espresso. It's a unique blend of science and art, and when you combine it with latte art, you get something that takes lots of time to get good at, but that many people underappreciate. Shop at local coffee spots and roasters, avoid chains.

So don't waste time! Get what you need, then turn off that computer and get out into the great outdoors!
